A Marketplace For Owners To Sell Or Lease Commercial Real Estate And Business

Newlista helps you sell Commercial Real Estate for Sale by Owner. At Newlista, you can list your commercial or business real estate for sale without the middleman. We also offer Commercial Property for Lease by Owner, depending on your personal needs. We allow owners to create property listings for free and get notified immediately when a buyer makes an offer. You have the power to accept or decline offers and stay up to date in your personal portal. Your dashboard will provide updates on your property, your listing views, inquiries and allow easy communication with potential buyers. Businesses for sale by owner are also a popular listing for Newlista users. We accept various Commercial Real Estate Properties for your FSBO Commercial Real Estate needs.
